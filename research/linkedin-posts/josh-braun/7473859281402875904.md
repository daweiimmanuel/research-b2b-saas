Never confuse features and benefits again.

A feature is what you do:
We run Facebook ads for personal injury lawyers.

A benefit is what they can do:
(Audio clip of a real lead)
"My name is Mike. I hurt my back and was wondering if you could help me out."

For $2,000 per month, we'll generate 23–30 inbound calls like this from people living in Florida actively looking for help with back pain.

Features describe the vehicle.
Benefits describe the destination.
