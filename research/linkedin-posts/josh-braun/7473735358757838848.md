You can try to get wealthy trading crypto.

Or…

Pay yourself first.

Invest 22% of every paycheck into a low-cost ETF.

(The average person saves about 4%.)

Oh, and while you're at it…

Drive a boring car for 10 years.

A Honda.
A Camry.
Something that starts every morning and doesn't require a personality.

No charts.
No Discord groups.
No "this coin is about to 100x."

Just boring consistency.

Boring has a much better track record than exciting.

Most people want financial freedom.

They also want the journey to be entertaining.

That's usually where things get expensive.

Most fortunes are built slowly and reported suddenly.
